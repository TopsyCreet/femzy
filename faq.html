Sizing & Fit

Visit our Size Guide page for detailed measurements across all product categories including tops, hoodies, jackets, pants, shorts, and headwear. We include both cm and inch measurements.
Most of our pieces are designed with an oversized streetwear fit. If you prefer a more fitted look, we recommend sizing down. Check each product's description for specific fit notes, or reach out to us and we'll advise you directly.
If you're between two sizes, we recommend going one size up for a relaxed streetwear silhouette. For a snug or structured fit, go one size down. When in doubt, message us on WhatsApp — we're happy to help you decide.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unworn, unwashed, and in its original condition with tags intact. Sale items and headwear are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
To exchange an item, contact us within 7 days of receiving your order via WhatsApp or email. State your order number, the item you received, and the size or colour you'd like instead. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.
We're sorry if this happens! Please contact us immediately with your order number and a photo of the item. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you.

Product Care

To keep your pieces looking fresh:
  • Cold wash only (30°C or below)
  • Turn garments inside out before washing
  • Do not tumble dry — air dry flat
  • Iron inside out on a low heat setting
  • Do not bleach or dry clean
Following these steps preserves the colour, print quality, and shape of your Alpha gear.
Our garments are made with high-quality, colour-fast fabrics. With proper care (cold wash, no tumble dry), your pieces will maintain their vibrancy wash after wash. Avoid harsh detergents and prolonged exposure to direct sunlight when drying.
