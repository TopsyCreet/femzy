Size Guide

Find your perfect fit. All measurements are in centimetres (cm) unless toggled to inches.

How to Measure Yourself

📏

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal under your arms.

📐

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso.

↕️

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 20cm below your waistline.

📍

Inseam

Measure from your crotch seam to the bottom of your leg along the inner thigh.

🔵

Shoulders

Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the other across the back.

💡

Pro Tip

Use a soft measuring tape and wear fitted clothing. If between sizes, size up for a relaxed fit.

Between Sizes?

We always recommend sizing up for a more relaxed, streetwear silhouette. Size down for a fitted, structured look.

Washing & Care

Cold wash only. Do not tumble dry. Iron inside out on low heat. This preserves the shape and print quality of your Alpha gear.
